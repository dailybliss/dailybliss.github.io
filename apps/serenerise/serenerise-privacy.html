Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 17 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SereneRise — Brahma Muhurta Time & Widget ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Introduction

SereneRise is developed by DailyBliss. This policy covers what data the App uses, how it's stored, and your rights as a user.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data

The App requests access to your device location to calculate accurate Brahma Muhurta and sunrise times for your area. Specifically:

  • Foreground location ("While using the app") — used to calculate times when the app is open.
  • Background location ("Allow all the time") — used to automatically update the home screen widget with accurate times based on your current location, even when the app is closed.
  • Location data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Location is never transmitted to any external server.
  • Location is only updated when you move more than 50 kilometres from your previously saved location.

Device Information

  • Basic technical data such as device type and operating system version may be collected to improve performance.
  • Crash logs may be collected to identify and fix issues.

Subscription Data

  • If you purchase a subscription, Google Play processes payment information.
  • The App itself does not access or store your payment details.
  • Subscription status is verified locally via Google Play Billing.

No Personal Data

The App does not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

3. How We Use Information

  • To calculate and display accurate Brahma Muhurta times for your location.
  • To automatically refresh the home screen widget hourly with updated times.
  • To update widget content when date changes at midnight.
  • To verify subscription status through Google Play Billing.
  • To improve app stability and performance.

4. Data Storage

  • All data including location, subscription status, and preferences is stored locally on your device only.
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on any external server or cloud service.
  • The App functions completely offline — no internet connection is required except for subscription purchase and verification.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent user information.
  • We do not share location data with any third party.
  • Subscription and payment processing are handled securely by Google Play Billing.

6. Background Location

The App requests background location permission solely to update the home screen widget with accurate Brahma Muhurta times when you travel to a new location. This permission is optional — if you prefer not to grant it, the widget will continue to function using your last saved location. You can update your location manually by opening the app.

Background location data is:

  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Only used for local sunrise time calculation
  • Only accessed when a significant location change (50km+) is detected

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences storage. No sensitive data is transmitted over the internet.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App, on the Play Store listing, and the effective date will be updated. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
