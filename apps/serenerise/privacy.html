Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 April 2026

SereneRise is designed to respect your privacy. This page explains, in plain English, what data the app uses, what it doesn't, and why.

Summary

  • SereneRise does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server.
  • The app uses your device location only to compute Brahma Muhurta times locally on your device.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not require an account, email, or sign-in.

What data the app uses

Device location

SereneRise requests location permission so it can calculate the correct sunrise — and therefore the correct Brahma Muhurta window — for your exact geographic position. The calculation happens entirely on your device. Your location is never sent to us or to any third party.

You can revoke location permission at any time through your device's system settings. The app will continue to work, but you'll need to manually enter coordinates or use a previously cached location.

Cached preferences

The app stores your preferences (such as time format, last-known location, and widget settings) in local app storage on your device. This information never leaves your device.

What the app does not do

  • It does not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifying information.
  • It does not transmit your location to any server.
  • It does not use advertising identifiers or show ads.
  • It does not include analytics SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics or Google Analytics).
  • It does not track you across apps or websites.

Permissions explained

  • Location (approximate or precise): Used solely to calculate sunrise times for your location.
  • Internet access: Not required for core functionality. Requested only so the app can fetch the latest astronomical data if needed.

Children's privacy

SereneRise does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since the app collects no personal information at all, it is safe for users of any age.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this page and update the "Last updated" date above. For material changes, a notice will appear in the app itself.
