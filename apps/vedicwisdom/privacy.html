Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 April 2026

Vedic Wisdom is designed to respect your privacy. This page explains, in plain English, what data the app uses, what it doesn't, and why.

Summary

  • Vedic Wisdom does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server.
  • The app's quote content is bundled within the app; no network request is needed to display it.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not require an account, email, or sign-in.

What data the app uses

Local preferences

The app stores your preferences (such as preferred sources — Bhagavad Gita, Upanishads, Vivekananda, Buddha, Kabir — widget settings, and the date of the last-shown quote) in local app storage on your device. This information never leaves your device.

Quote content

All quotes are bundled with the app itself. The app does not need internet access to function. When you update the app through the Play Store, new or revised quotes may be included with the update.

What the app does not do

  • It does not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifying information.
  • It does not require or request your location.
  • It does not use advertising identifiers or show ads.
  • It does not include analytics SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics or Google Analytics).
  • It does not track you across apps or websites.

Permissions explained

Vedic Wisdom requests no permissions beyond what is required for a standard home-screen widget to display on your device.

Children's privacy

Vedic Wisdom does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since the app collects no personal information at all, it is safe for users of any age.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this page and update the "Last updated" date above. For material changes, a notice will appear in the app itself.
