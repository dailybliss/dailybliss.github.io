How it works

Search for any city (or use your device location), and the tool calculates Brahma Muhurta for every day of the coming year. The times are computed using the standard NOAA solar position algorithm — entirely in your browser, nothing sent to a server.

Brahma Muhurta begins 96 minutes before sunrise (two muhurtas) and lasts 48 minutes. Since sunrise shifts throughout the year, the window can vary by over an hour between summer and winter, depending on your latitude.

Use Share Calendar to download a CSV file with all 365 days — it opens in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app.
